## Abstract Submission

We are soliciting abstract submissions for talks to be presented at this splinter session, on topics related to substellar (brown dwarfs/giant exoplanets) atmospheres, from both a theoretical and modeling perspective, in addition to observations, with a focus on science enabled by JWST.

Each selected contributed talk will be allocated 12 minutes + 3 minutes for questions.

If you are not selected for a talk and/or you are presenting a poster, there is also an opportunity to give a poster-pop (1-2min talk) during the splinter session.

Submit your abstract [here](https://forms.gle/Z8KQ4SSzjCrB1Fd86).
